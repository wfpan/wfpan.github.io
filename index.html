About me

I am an Associate Professor in the School of Computer Science and Information Engineering at the Zhejiang Gongshang University. I have been a visiting scholar at Western Michigan University, working with Prof. Zijiang James Yang (杨子江).

You can contact me at remove-wfpan@zjgsu.edu.cn or remove-panweifeng1982@gmail.com. (Delete ‘remove-‘ to use these email addresses.)

I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Software and Theory from School of Computer at Wuhan University (WHU) in 2011 under the supervision of Prof. Bing Li (李兵).

My current research interests include software engineering, service computing, complex networks, and intelligent computation. I am a member of China Computer Federation (CCF), CCF Technical Committee on Services Computing (CCF TCSC), and Association for Computing Machinery (ACM).
